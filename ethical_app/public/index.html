About us

We are a cooperative enterprise dedicated to building an ethical online market place (ethicalBay) that is owned and controlled by its producers and consumers.

Be the Good:Buy the Good

We want to make it easy and simple for producers and consumers to sell and “buy the good” : ethical products.

Our Vision

  • A global community of ethical producers and consumers connected by a cooperatively owned online marketplace.

  • We’re calling it ethicalBay: the place where people meet to exchange not only value but also Values.

  • Imagine if Amazon, eBay or Alibaba were actually owned by the people who trade through them and those people create and trade goods and services using resources, processes and supply chains that are sustainable and do no harm to people or planet.

  • How good would that be?

We The Workers

  • The assets of the business are owned and controlled by the people who do the work, who create and equitably share the value.

  • Workers cooperatives, partnerships, sole traders, co-owned businesses.

Do No Harm

The provisioning, creation and supply processes should aim to minimise harm to flora and fauna, people and planet.

Sustainable

  • At the cosmic level nothing lasts forever, in time everything decays but whilst the sun is shining we can adapt and develop the creation and supply processes to sustain all life - including Gaia herself.
